System hangs while moving the mouse around on the screen

This information applies to the following system configurations:

  • ATI Proprietary Linux Driver 3.14.1 or earlier

While moving the mouse around on the screen, the system will hang. This will happen just when the mouse cursor image is about to change.

This issue is most likely to occur when numerous windows are open and many things are happening on the screen simultaneously.

This issue has been resolved in the 3.14.6 hotfix driver.
