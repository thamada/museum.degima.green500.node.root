XFree86 crashes with RADEON 9600 on systems with Intel Springdale chipset

This information applies to the following:

  • ATI Proprietary Linux Driver
  • RADEON 9600 Series graphics cards
  • Systems using Springdale (i865) chipset

The system will hang when attempting to launch XFree86.

This issue has been resolved in ATI Proprietary Linux Driver version 3.12 and later.
