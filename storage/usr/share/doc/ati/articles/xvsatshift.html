Adjusting colour saturation during movie playback results in colour shift

This information applies to the following:

  • ATI Proprietary Linux Driver
  • Xv overlay enabled for video playback

Many video playback applications provide a setting for colour saturation. When changing this setting to relatively high values during movie playback, the colour in the picture may shift to green or yellow.

This only occurs if the Xv overlay is enabled, which is true by default, and if the video playback application takes advantage of Xv video acceleration. It only occurs at the high end of the saturation spectrum.

This issue has been resolved in ATI Proprietary Linux Driver version 3.14 and later.
